Naan

Ingredients

  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 ½ cups bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic (optional)
  • ¼ cup melted butter (for brushing)

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it stand for 10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix sugar, milk, egg, and salt into the yeast mixture.
  3. Add enough flour to make a soft dough and knead for 6–8 minutes on a lightly floured surface.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise until doubled in size (about 1 hour).
  5. Punch down dough, knead in garlic if using, and divide into 14 small balls. Let rise again for 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat a grill pan or skillet on high heat.
  7. Roll each ball into a thin circle.
  8. Brush melted butter on the grill and cook naan until bubbles form and it browns (2–3 mins per side).
  9. Brush with more butter after flipping and remove when done.
  10. Serve warm and enjoy!

Tips

  • For garlic naan, brush the naan with garlic butter instead of kneading it in.
  • Can be stored in an airtight bag at room temp for up to 3 days.
  • Reheat by sprinkling with water and warming in the oven.
